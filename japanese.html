Popular Japanese Dishes

Sushi

Description: A traditional Japanese dish with vinegared rice, raw fish, and vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 3 cups water
  • 1/3 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Fresh fish (e.g., salmon, tuna)
  • Seaweed sheets (nori)
  • Vegetables (e.g., cucumber, avocado)
  • Soy sauce and wasabi (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the sushi rice thoroughly and cook it with water in a rice cooker.
  2. Mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt until dissolved, then fold it into the cooked rice while it's still warm.
  3. Lay a sheet of nori on a bamboo rolling mat.
  4. Spread a thin layer of rice on the nori, leaving a small border at the top edge.
  5. Add slices of fish and vegetables in a line near the bottom edge.
  6. Roll the mat tightly from the bottom, pressing gently to form a cylinder.
  7. Slice the roll into bite-sized pieces and serve with soy sauce and wasabi.

Ramen

Description: Noodles in a savory broth topped with pork, egg, and vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups chicken or pork broth
  • 2 packs fresh ramen noodles
  • 2 boiled eggs
  • 4 slices chashu pork (or any cooked pork belly)
  • 1 cup sliced green onions
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon miso paste
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat the broth in a pot and stir in soy sauce, miso paste, and sesame oil. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  2. Cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions and drain.
  3. Divide the noodles into bowls and pour the hot broth over them.
  4. Top with chashu pork, boiled eggs, green onions, and corn kernels.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
